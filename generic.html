University of Washington

University of Washington (UW) is a public research university in Seattle, Washington

History

The University was founded in 1861 and it has over 500 buildings and over 20 million gross square footage of space.

Seattle Campus

The 703-acre main Seattle campus is situated in the University District above the Montlake Cut.

Odegaard Library

Odegaard Library is the one of the main libraries on campus.

About the Company

This is a company that is completely initiated by students from University of Washington and it has been supporting the propaganda of the school and Seattle for two years.

The sponsors for the company are from University of Washington and other Seattle area.
